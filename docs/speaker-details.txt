1st Speaker
Topic Details - “The SaaS Blueprint: Building in the Clouds”

This session dives into the anatomy of Software as a Service — exploring how SaaS applications are built, hosted, and delivered via the cloud. Attendees will learn about key concepts like multi-tenancy, deployment models, and the role of APIs and microservices in building scalable software. The talk will unpack how SaaS simplifies complexity for users while powering innovation behind the scenes.

2nd Speaker
Topic Details - “Scaling Heights: The Art of Seamless Growth”

In this talk, students will explore how modern SaaS platforms achieve flexibility and performance at scale. The speaker will discuss elastic cloud infrastructure, load balancing, containerization (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes), and CI/CD pipelines — showing how small ideas can grow to serve thousands. Attendees will also gain insight into real-world cloud scaling challenges and how engineers solve them efficiently.

3rd Speaker
Topic Details - “Innovation on Demand: SaaS and the Startup Mindset”

How can a single idea turn into a global SaaS product? This talk explores the entrepreneurial side of the SaaS revolution. The speaker will share their journey of founding or scaling a SaaS-based startup — including lessons on go-to-market strategies, customer retention, and leveraging cloud tools to build lean yet powerful digital products. This session aims to inspire students to innovate, collaborate, and launch ideas that ascend beyond boundaries.
